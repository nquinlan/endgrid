What I Learned

  • Always be yourself.
  • Be kind.
  • Random acts of generosity go far.
  • Help others unconditionally.
  • High fives are gold.
  • Always explode a fist bump.
  • Keep in touch with your friends and the people you meet.
  • Identify what you’re good at and work there.
  • Identify what you’re bad at and improve.
  • Keep mentors.
  • Numbers matter. Metrics are invaluable.

In my year-and-a-halfish at SendGrid, I learned more than I had ever learned before. I traveled the country and the world. I met friends and found mentors. Coworkers became friends. Friends became family. I worked on the best evangelist team in the world at an amazing company. It was spectacular.
